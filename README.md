# marketplaceCalc

**Functions:**
```
1- Add your products with its price
2- Modify its name price or remove the product from the application
3- GUI of the product added to the shopping cart, with the price before and after tax
4- When you click on cash, it adds to the cash total/cash without tax, so you can post it after your shift the total
5- The ability to reset cash total/cash without tax
6- When clicked on debit/credit, it erases the shopping cart
7- The cash total/cash without tax and the products (name, price) saved after you close the application on a .txt file that it creates automatically
```
